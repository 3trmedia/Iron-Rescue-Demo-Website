Mass Casualty Trauma Response

Train to Save Lives Before Medical Help Arrives

When seconds count, your training is the only thing that matters.

When something goes wrong, most people freeze. You want to be different. The Mass Casualty Trauma Response course gives everyday people the hands-on medical skills to step in, take action, and keep someone alive until professional help arrives.

Train With Us See Course Dates

What You'll Master

Life Saving Skills
Learn the critical interventions that keep someone alive until rescue arrives, even in the most chaotic and high-pressure situations.
Hands-On Training
Practice every skill repeatedly until it becomes muscle memory under pressure. We run realistic scenarios so training translates directly to real events.
TECC Certification
Earn nationally recognized Tactical Emergency Casualty Care certification upon completion — a credential that carries weight in any emergency response situation.
Medical Scenarios
Train in realistic multi-casualty situations — accidents, mass injury events, and emergencies so nothing catches you off guard when it matters most.
Bleeding Simulators
Learn to pack wounds, apply tourniquets, and use hemostatic gauze to stop massive bleeding fast. These are the skills that buy critical minutes.
Medical Tools
Leave with a complete Individual First Aid Kit configured specifically for responding to traumatic emergencies wherever they happen.

Course Structure

A Straightforward Process That Turns Caring, Untrained People Into Confident First Responders.

1
Learn The Material
We start with the medical knowledge that actually makes a difference — how trauma affects the body, what needs immediate attention, and how to stay calm and focused when someone needs you to be. No jargon, no overwhelm. Just what matters.
2
Practice The Skills
Understanding something and being able to do it are two different things. You'll work through realistic hands-on scenarios — controlling bleeding on simulators, stabilizing injuries, making quick decisions under pressure with instructors coaching you every step of the way. By the end, these skills aren't just learned. They're yours.
3
Acquire The Gear
Every participant walks away with a complete Individual First Aid Kit, nationally recognized TECC certification, and access to future training. You came in wanting to help. You'll leave knowing you can.
